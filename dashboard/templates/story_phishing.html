1. The Setup Compromised Account · Phishing
⚠ ATTACKER
Eve
Employee account (stolen via phishing link) · 2024-04-02 · 14:08
An outsider who tricked an employee into clicking a phishing link and harvested their credentials. They log in from 198.51.100.42 (a rented VPS, also their exfil server) after 2 failed password attempts, then sweep 4 files across HR and Finance — outside the employee's normal role — pack them with 7z.exe, and exfiltrate 68 MB to the same IP 26 minutes later. The attack is fast, quiet, and looks almost indistinguishable from a business traveler.
✓ INNOCENT — FALSE POSITIVE TARGET
Frank
Employee · Business trip · Hotel WiFi · 2024-04-02 · 14:14
An employee on a business trip logging in from the hotel lobby. He mistyped his password 3 times on the hotel keyboard, then worked normally all afternoon through the hotel's WiFi proxy (198.18.100.22 — a non-mainstream IP range, just like Eve's VPS). Same new IP, same login hour (14:xx), same destination IP category — SIEM cannot tell them apart.
