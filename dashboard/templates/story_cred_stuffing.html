1. The Setup Compromised Account · Credential Stuffing
⚠ ATTACKER
Carol
IT account (compromised by outsider) · 2024-03-15 · Daytime attack
An external attacker has obtained a credential list targeting IT staff accounts. They try 9 passwords against carol's account in 6 minutes from 185.220.101.42 (a rented VPS), get in on attempt #9, then immediately run mimikatz.exe, sweep 8 files across IT and HR directories in 5 minutes, archive them, and exfiltrate 680 MB to the same VPS — all in a 90-minute window during business hours.
✓ INNOCENT — FALSE POSITIVE TARGET
Dave
Support user · Working from home · 2024-03-15
A support employee working from home. He hasn't changed his VPN password in months and forgot it — failing 15 times over 2.7 hours before finally getting in at 11:32 AM. Like carol, he logs in from a new IP (his home network) at the same hour. He then does routine support work and goes home. No suspicious network activity whatsoever.

XDR Thesis — What This Case Proves
